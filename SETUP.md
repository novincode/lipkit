## Quick Start

**Create mouth shapes (9 layers/keys named):**
- `X` - Closed mouth
- `A`, `B`, `C`, `D`, `E`, `F`, `G`, `H` - Different mouth shapes

**Generate:**
1. Load audio
2. Click "Analyze Audio"
3. Select your mouth object
4. Click "Generate Lip Sync"

That's it!
